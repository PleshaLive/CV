About Me

I am an experienced esports broadcast director with over 10 years in live production, specializing in tournaments, world championships, and major events. Confident in working with Blackmagic and vMix, I have deep expertise in studio switching, production workflows, and communication within high-pressure live environments.

Since 2014, I’ve worked across the full spectrum of esports broadcasting-commentator, observer, sound engineer, director, operator, and producer. My portfolio includes directing flagship events such as the WePlay AniMajor and most WePlay tournaments since 2020, as well as the CS:GO Major 2019 with Starladder. I’ve also introduced innovative features to esports production, including custom textures in CS:GO (team gloves at MLG Major) and in-game 3D model integration via SFM and Unity.

I offer professional live streaming services for streamers, online tournaments, and events of any scale-covering every detail from observing, graphics, and sound to replays, effects, and advanced broadcast features. My hands-on approach ensures polished, audience-focused productions, whether for high-stakes tournaments, community events, or creative projects.
